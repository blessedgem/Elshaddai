How we started ?

GemSoft is a final year project work

It is aimed at connecting Big Data System such as Apache Hadoop with the traditional database management systems.

It is an open source software

And we are still under development and will complete soon.

Purpose Of GemSoft

Aid organisations to integrate their traditional relational database with hadoop

How To Use GemSoft

You could either start by clicking on Database to load a specific database from a specific RDBMS or click on Hadoop to export a processed data.
